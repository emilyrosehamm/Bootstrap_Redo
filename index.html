Who am I

Hello! Welcome to my page I am Emily Anaclerio-Hammel. A driven designer committed to creating user centric designs to improve functionality and user experience, while maintaining company authenticity. A recent graduate of the UX/UI program at University of Texas Austin. While involved in many projects, I also worked as project manager keeping the team on task. A background in photography instilled a keen eye for details and a strong pull to composition and color. Instrumental to the rebranding effort with previous employer, responsible for creation and implementation of new workflows for back-end and front-end procedures.
